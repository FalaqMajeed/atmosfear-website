Meet the Atmosfear Team

Passionate students from the MIT Emerging Talent Program working to make air pollution's health impacts visible and actionable through innovative data science.

About Atmosfear

Atmosfear investigates the global health impacts of air pollution, focusing on how socioeconomic disparities shape vulnerability. We use data science to uncover hidden patterns and make complex research accessible through our interactive tools.

We want to humanize the numbers and make the invisible visible.
